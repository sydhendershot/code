WHAT’S YOUR TYPICAL PROCESS?

Prior to your appointment, you’ll fill out a new client profile form including your sizing, lifestyle, frustrations, goals, etc.


We’ll schedule a short call to answer any questions and further explain our process.


Next, we’ll book the first step: a Closet Edit.

CLOSET EDIT:

Your stylist will come to your home and you’ll start by reviewing your wardrobe, typically piece by piece. Your stylist will help you decide what you should keep, repurpose, tailor, consign or donate. During the process, we’ll take notes and formulate your shopping checklist. Next, you’ll determine together whether a shopping service and/or outfit styling session would be helpful. *Virtual options are available.

STRATEGIC SHOPPING:

We offer multiple methods for Personal Shopping. For online shopping, your stylist will send you links to item recommendations on your own Virtual Storefront. For ‘store-to-door’ in-home shopping, your stylist will order items and bring them to your home at a designated time for a try-on session. For in-store shopping, your stylist will go to the store ahead of you and have items ready and waiting in a fitting room. You’ll meet at the designated store and try on the items selected for you.



OUTFIT STYLING:

Once we’ve edited your closet (and shopped for any missing pieces), it’s often best to come full circle in the put-together process and have your stylist show you what goes with what. In this session, we’ll work with your existing wardrobe to create new outfits. We’ll lay out every look and photograph them for you.
