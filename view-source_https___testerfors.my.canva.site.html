CLOSET EDIT:

Your stylist will come to your home and youll start by reviewing your wardrobe, typically piece by piece. Your stylist will help you decide what you should keep, repurpose, tailor, consign or donate. During the process, well take notes and formulate your shopping checklist. Next, youll determine together whether a shopping service and/or outfit styling session would be helpful. *Virtual options are available.

STRATEGIC SHOPPING:

We offer multiple methods for Personal Shopping. For online shopping, your stylist will send you links to item recommendations on your own Virtual Storefront. For store-to-door in-home shopping, your stylist will order items and bring them to your home at a designated time for a try-on session. For in-store shopping, your stylist will go to the store ahead of you and have items ready and waiting in a fitting room. Youll meet at the designated store and try on the items selected for you.



OUTFIT STYLING:

Once weve edited your closet (and shopped for any missing pieces), its often best to come full circle in the put-together process and have your stylist show you what goes with what. In this session, well work with your existing wardrobe to create new outfits. Well lay out every look and photograph them for you.
